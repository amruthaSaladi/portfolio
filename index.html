Women Kavach Band

This is an IoT based device, where it consists of 2 devices: Kavach band and a companion device. Companion device produces a timely alerts to the Trusted ones. Marine Oil Spills Detection

This is the detection of oil spills in the marine environements through analysis of Hyperspectral images of Gulf of Mexico ocean. The steps include: Preprocesing and segementation. Preprocessing include removal of bad bands and reduction of dimensionality of data.
